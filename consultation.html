Skype Consultation Q&A

Q: How to request a skype consultation?

A: Send me an email titled "Skype Consultation Request". In the email, specify your top 3 half-hour slots by preference, allowing two weeks notice. Attach a text file named "Request". The "Request" file will be a text/word file detailing your request - what problem I can help you resolve. Also attach any resources you would like me to look at.

I or my assistant will reply to your request either confirming a time and sending you a payment request to your e-mail thru PayPal, or letting you know why your request was denied. Please make sure to provide payment a week in advance of our consultation.

Q: Can more than one person join the Skype call during our hour-long session?

A: Yes.

Q: What if we experience technical difficulty during the Skype call?

A: You will be fully credited for any time lost. We can continue the conversation over the phone or reschedule another call.

Q: What if we prefer a teleconference or phone call instead of a Skype call?

A: That’s fine with me and the rates are the same.

Q: What if we have additional questions before deciding whether or not to use your services?

A: Please email me and I will be happy to address any add’l questions you may have. I look forward to hearing from you. Best regards!
